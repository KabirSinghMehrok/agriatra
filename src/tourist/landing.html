About us

Agriãtra specializes in providing an unique and immersive agritravel experiences. We offer a variety of agritourism activities for visitors of all ages, including guided tours of our organic gardens, farm-to-table cooking classes, and educational workshops on sustainable agriculture practices.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful getaway or just a a foodie looking to learn about sustainable agriculture, Agriãtra has something for everyone. Contact us today to book your agritourism package and start planning your perfect getaway.
