Chandgarh Farms is a beautiful location located in Dibugarh, Uttar Pradesh. The farm is surrounded by lush green fields and forests, providing a peaceful and serene environment for visitors to enjoy.

One of the main attractions of Chandgarh Farms is the beautiful mango orchards that are found there. The orchards are filled with a variety of mango trees, producing a delicious and juicy fruit that is loved by people all over the world. The orchards are carefully tended to by skilled farmers, ensuring that the mangoes are of the highest quality.

In addition to the mango orchards, Chandgarh Farms also offers a variety of activities for visitors to enjoy. These include nature walks, bird watching, and picnics in the beautiful surroundings.

Overall, Chandgarh Farms is a fantastic place to visit for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and enjoy the natural beauty of Uttar Pradesh. The beautiful mango orchards and peaceful setting make it a perfect destination for a relaxing and enjoyable day out.
